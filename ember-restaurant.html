EST. 2014 — OPEN-FLAME KITCHEN

We cook the way fire intended.

Ember started as a single charcoal grill behind a butcher's shop — the kind of fire you light at dawn and never let die. Years later the grill is bigger and the dining room is quieter, but the rule hasn't changed: nothing reaches your table that hasn't first met the flame.

Our cooks read coals the way a sommelier reads a glass — by smell, by sound, by the way the smoke bends. We season early, we wait, and we serve only when the fire says it's ready. Not before.

  • No. 01 Live-fire only. No gas, ever.
  • No. 02 Coals are split and lit fresh every service.
  • No. 03 The counter seats six; the dining room seats sixty.
